Odin Recipes



It’s a shame that the standard way of learning how to cook is by following recipes.To be sure,
they are a wonderfully effective way to approximate a dish as it appeared in a test kitchen,
at a star chef’s restaurant, or on TV.
And they can be an excellent inspiration for even the least ambitious home cooks to liven up a weeknight dinner.
But recipes, for all their precision and completeness, are poor teachers. They tell you what to do, but they rarely tell you why to do it.
