Chocolate Moose

This nontraditional dessert will require a slightly larger refrigerator.

This is for VERY special occasions only â€” it takes a lot of effort, but the presentation is spectacular!"

READY IN: 80hrs

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Moose

  • 40kg Chocolate chips

  • 25 Cans of Whipped cream

  • 1 Jar of Cherries

DIRECTIONS
  1. Go to Alaska to capture moose, or have one delivered by post.

  2. Meanwhile, melt chocolate in very large stew pot

  3. Keep warm until served

  4. Tie up moose with rope.

  5. Holding the moose by the tail, carefully dip in melted chocolate, covering it completely with a thin coating.

  6. Arrange moose attractively on large platter and refrigerate for 2 days to set chocolate.

  7. Remove rope, wash to remove chocolate,if necessary, and return rope to wash line.

  8. Garnish chocolate moose with Cool Whip and top with a cherry.

  9. Serve immediately.

  10. Or you could just chew on the rope, which may be tastier.
